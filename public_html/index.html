About Me

My Name is John Romero and I was born and raised right here in Albuquerque. I have been in the Healthcare field for 13 years now. I strive to educate my fellow New Mexican's as much as I can about their healthcare and the options that are available to them. It is not my job to tell you which plan I think is best but to help you make a decision, that you think is best for your health for now and the future.
