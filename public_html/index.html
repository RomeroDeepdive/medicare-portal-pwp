Medicare Basics

Prescription Drug Coverage

When you sign up for Medicare you must either purchase a Part D drug plan or get a Part C/Medicare Advantage which will combine your prescription coverage with medical and no additional cost.

Part A Medicare

As long as you work/paid taxes for 10 years, part A hospital coverage is something you will automaically receive upon turning 65.

Medicare Part B

Medicare Part B is what you must sign up for either 3 months prior to your birth month, the month of, or 3 months after your birth month.

Medicare and YOU

When just turning 65, you have 3 months before your birthday, the month of and then 3 months afterwards to sign up for a medicare plan. Please make sure you follow the paperwork to sign up for Part A and B. You can then contact me to assist you in finding a Medicare plan that can benefit you the most.
Typially as a Veteran you would want to use the V.A service's as a primary. Now, one thing you can certainly take advantage of are the additional benefits that come with a Medicare plan, such as gym memberships through Silver Sneakers, dental, vision and much more.
When you have been on disability benefits for 2 or more years and are under the age of 65 you can still wualify for these great medicare plans across the State. Many are unaware that they can have Medicare under the age of 65.
if you qualify for "full" medicaid and are on medicare, you wualify for a DSNP or dual plan. These policies maximize your healthcare benefits. You may qualify for $2,000 in dental, over $200 in over the counter benefits, transportation service's and more.
There are plans where you do not have to pay an additional premium for dental coverage from $100 up to $2,000.
With the over the counter benefit, you may purchase items every month from walgreens, CVS or over the phone with your OTC booklet. There are plans that offer anywhere from $25-$150 a month in OTC benefits. These items include, but not limited to tooth brushes, over the counter prescriptions, shower heads, medical supplies and much more.
